Help & Support

Library information, FAQs, and contact details for Mapúa University Library.

About the Library

Mapúa University Library — Intramuros

The Mapúa University Library has served students and faculty since the University's founding in 1925. Located at the 2nd Floor, West Building, 658 Muralla St., Intramuros, Manila, it is the central hub for academic research, digital resources, and collaborative learning.

The library provides access to thousands of physical books, e-journals, theses, databases, and multimedia resources covering Engineering, Computer Science, Architecture, Business, Medicine, and more. A branch library is also available in Makati for students enrolled at that campus.

Vision & Mission

Vision: Mapúa Library envisions itself as one of the country's leading academic libraries.

Mission: To support teaching, research, and extension functions of the Institution by assuring equitable access to high-quality information resources and services for all students, faculty, and researchers.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I borrow a book?

Present your valid Mapúa ID at the circulation desk. Through SmartLib, you can reserve a book via the Catalog page and pick it up at the library. Standard loan period is 3 days for regular books and 1 day for reserve items.

How do I renew or return a borrowed book?

Renewals can be done through your SmartLib "My Library" page or by visiting the circulation desk. Books must be returned before the due date to avoid fines. A return drop box is available at the library entrance.

Can outside researchers use the library?

Yes. External researchers must email the library (library@mapua.edu.ph) with a list of needed materials and bring a referral letter from their institution. A maximum of 16 visiting users are accommodated per day.

How do I access e-books and online databases?

Log in to SmartLib with your Mapúa student account. Databases such as IEEE Xplore, Wiley Online Library, and SAGE are accessible through the library portal. Off-campus access may require VPN credentials — contact the library for details.

How do I book a discussion room?

Discussion rooms at the Collaborative Zone can be reserved through the Mapúa LibCal system at mapua.libcal.com. Bookings are subject to availability and library operating hours.

What happens if I return a book late?

Overdue fines are charged per day per item. Unpaid fines may suspend your borrowing privileges. View your due dates and fines anytime under "My Library" in SmartLib.
